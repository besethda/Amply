Step 1 — Create Your Artist Environment

Amply will create your own private AWS environment for music uploads. This includes an S3 bucket, IAM role, and CloudFront delivery system — all automatically linked to Amply.

Once you click the button, AWS will open in a new tab. After the setup finishes, click the “Return to Amply” button in AWS.
